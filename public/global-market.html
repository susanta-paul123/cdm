Quality Legacy Powers Global Reach

We draw on over 250 years of manufacturing experience with horse care products coupled with a well-established distribution network delivering our range to customers around the world every day.

Since 1765, Carr & Day & Martin has been a leader in the manufacture of the best quality horse care products for horses worldwide. From as early as 1820, the range was granted a royal warrant in England due to its superior quality which is still part of its heritage, legacy & standards to the present day.

Nowadays. 90 products are produced all to the same high standard set respectively by Messrs Day & Martin in 1770 and Carr & Son in 1837 and have established Carr & Day & Martin firmly as a world-renowned leader in horse care & grooming distributed worldwide.

Global Markets Presence

For over 250 years, Carr Day Martin has been at the forefront of innovative horse care solutions, and our ever-growing global distribution and retail network is a testament to this reputation.

Our Centralised Manufacturing Facility in Ireland: Serving Horse Owners Worldwide

Carr Day Martin operates a central manufacturing facility in Ireland, which allows us to maintain our commitment to quality and innovation, ensuring our products are made to the highest standards.

Originally established in the UK, our business quickly diversified and expanded across Europe, where our products are sold in over 27 European countries, including France, Germany, and Italy. We have established a strong presence in North America, where we are sold extensively in the USA and Canada.

Strong Retail and Distribution Partnerships

At Carr Day Martin, we have built strong relationships with our partners and distributors across the globe. Our dedication to maintaining these partnerships has allowed us to provide our customers with a diverse range of high-quality horse care, leather care, hoof care, and grooming products.

We understand the importance of working closely with our partners to ensure that our products are readily available to horse owners worldwide. Through our established distribution channels, we are able to reach customers, across the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, South Africa, and multiple countries in Europe, the Middle East, and the Far East.
