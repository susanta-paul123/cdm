Enduring Legacy of Quality in Horse Care from the Beginning

Established over 250 years ago, Carr & Day & Martin boasts an exceptional heritage in the equestrian world. Our journey began back in 1765, with the launch of our enduring Belvoir Tack Conditioner soap in 1800, a formulation that has stood the test of time.

The pivotal moment came in 1820 when we were granted the esteemed Royal Warrant by King George, an honour that has been retained through the reigns of 8 monarchs in the United Kingdom.

Our Values

Heritage, Innovation, Quality & Caring for the World around us

Carr Day Martin embraces four core values that shape our approach to horse care:

  • 01

    Traditional Heritage

    With a long-standing legacy, our commitment to preserving tradition is paramount. Our valued tradition upholds time-honoured formulations and craftsmanship, ensuring our products reflect the rich history of equestrian care.

  • 02

    Innovative Adaptation

    Blending tradition with innovation, we continually strive to develop cutting-edge solutions for modern equestrian needs. By combining our deep-rooted knowledge with the latest advancements, we create products that meet the evolving demands of todayâ€™s riders while maintaining our commitment to authenticity.

  • 03

    Caring for our World

    We prioritise sustainability and environmental responsibility in every aspect of our operations. We are also deeply passionate about our charity partnerships and collaborations for the best welfare of all our equine companions.

  • 04

    Unparalleled Quality and Service

    Our unwavering commitment to quality shines through in every product we create. We meticulously test and evolve our products for quality and efficacy through the test of time. Complementing our exceptional products, we provide unparalleled customer service, through our care team.

Our Sub Brands Family

Belvoir, Cornuscrescine, Vanner & Prest, and Berknell stand as cherished heritage brands within the Carr Day Martin family, each boasting a rich legacy of exceptional horse care products meticulously developed over the years. Over time, we have expanded our offerings with the introduction of newer sub-brands like Gallop Shampoo range and Canter Mane & Tail, along with FlyGard.
