Why CDM - Overview

Home About CDM Overview

A Quality Brand in a Sustainable & Caring Environment

Carr & Day & Martin stands as an exceptional quality brand because of its long-standing heritage of producing top-notch horse care products for over 250 years, fostering a deep understanding of the needs of horses and their owners.

Our customer reviews are a testament to the quality and effectiveness of our range from the beginning of time.

As a brand we place a strong emphasis on sustainability, striving to reduce its environmental impact through sustainable manufacturing practices, packaging, and products themselves. Furthermore, we have established enduring charity partnerships with both HAPPA and World Horse Welfare.

Learn More

Local Charity and Global Partnerships to Make a Difference

Uniting with Charities for Equine Welfare locally and abroad

Carr Day Martin is dedicated to supporting equine welfare through partnerships with both local and global charities. We have worked closely with HAPPA and World Horse Welfare for several years, providing care products that promote equine health and well-being.

As part of our #YOUCAREWECARE initiative, we plan to expand our support to other equine charities across the UK, Ireland, Germany, and the USA.

Our goal is to become the “Official Care Partner” of these organisations offering product support, training, education, and fundraising.
